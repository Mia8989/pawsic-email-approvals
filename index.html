How to Review

We have designed 8 email templates across three categories: Newsletters, Course Engagement, and Welcome emails. Each card below shows:

  1. A live preview of the email on the left (scroll to see the full email)
  2. Details about who receives this email and its purpose on the right
  3. A feedback form where you can approve or request changes

Simply type your name, add any comments, and click "Approve" or "Request Changes" for each email. Your feedback will be sent directly to the PAWSIC team. You can review on your phone or computer.
